In 1853, German immigrant Henry Engelhard Steinway founded Steinway & Sons in a small loft on Varick Street in Manhattan, New York. Over the next 30 years, Henry and his sons write a new chapter in modern piano manufacturing. They built pianos using handcrafted techniques passed down from master to apprentice. Today Steinway pianos are the preferred choice of more than 97% of pianists worldwide and are the dream piano and collector's item that everyone wants to own.
